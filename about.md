**nullstellensatz** is a math-exposition blog built for the
[Summer of Math Exposition](https://some.3b1b.co/). It collects careful,
example-first walkthroughs of ideas around the *polynomial method* — from
Cauchy–Davenport and Erdős–Heilbronn to Alon's Combinatorial Nullstellensatz.

The goal is simple: take theorems that usually arrive fully formed and rebuild
them from small, playable examples, so the "aha" is earned rather than asserted.

Written by **pi625**. The source lives on
[GitHub](https://github.com/pi625/nullstellensatz); math is typeset with MathJax.
