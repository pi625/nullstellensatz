Welcome. nullstellensatz is a small corner of the internet for math exposition — the kind of writing that takes a beautiful theorem and slows it down until you can see exactly why it has to be true.

The through-line here is the polynomial method: the surprising idea that counting the roots of a well-chosen polynomial can settle questions about sets, sums, and structure. We start with elementary sumset puzzles and build all the way to Alon's Combinatorial Nullstellensatz.

This site is my entry for the Summer of Math Exposition. Have a look at the writing.
